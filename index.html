What is Streaming?

Streaming refers to a multimedia file such as audio and video that can be instantly played without the
need to download it first. A media content, recorded or live, can be considered a streamed content as
long as it can be played on any device via the internet even if you do not download it. It works through
a continuous transmission of the media content from a server to the client.
